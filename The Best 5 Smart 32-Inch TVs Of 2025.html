Best 5 32-inch Smart TVs in India (2025)

In 2025, smart TVs have evolved beyond basic streaming capabilities, offering a perfect blend of performance, aesthetics, and intelligent features — even in compact sizes. For those looking to upgrade a bedroom, kitchen, or small apartment space, a 32-inch smart TV strikes the ideal balance between size and functionality. Whether you're a binge-watcher, casual viewer, or someone who values crisp visuals and seamless connectivity, the right 32-inch model can enhance your entertainment experience without overwhelming your space or budget.In this blog, we’ve handpicked the top 5 best 32-inch smart TVs of 2025, considering picture quality, user interface, app support, audio performance, and overall value. Let’s dive into the standout models this year has to offer.

1. Samsung UA32T4450AKXXL (32″, T4450 Series)

Buy on Amazon

The Samsung UA32T4450AKXXL is a 32-inch HD Ready (1366×768) Smart LED TV. It features Samsung’s HyperReal Picture Engine with HDR10, PurColor and Ultra Clean View technologies for enhanced color and contrast. It runs on Samsung’s Tizen smart TV platform, supporting popular apps and voice assistants (Amazon Alexa and Google Assistant). Audio is powered by dual speakers (2×10W) with Dolby Digital Plus, totaling 20W output. Connectivity includes 2 HDMI ports and 1 USB port, along with Wi‑Fi and Bluetooth for screen mirroring.

  • Key Features:
  • 32″ LED display (HD Ready, 1366×768) with HDR10, PurColor and Ultra Clean View.
  • Tizen OS smart platform with built-in apps and voice control (Alexa, Google).
  • Dolby Digital Plus audio (20W total) and slim “Mold” design.
  • 2 HDMI and 1 USB ports; Bluetooth 4.2 and Wi-Fi for wireless streaming.

Pros

  • Vibrant picture processing: HDR/PurColor enhances color and clarity.
  • Modern smart features: Tizen OS works with Alexa/Google voice control.
  • Robust audio: Dolby Digital Plus gives clear 20W sound.

Cons

  • Limited resolution: only HD Ready (1366×768).
  • Basic entry-level panel (no Full HD or local dimming).
  • Only 1 USB port; no built-in Bluetooth soundbar support (Bluetooth only for audio out).

2. LG 32LQ571CBSA (32″, LQ571 Series)

Buy on Amazon

The LG 32LQ571CBSA is a 32-inch Full HD (1920×1080) Smart LED TV, making it unique on this list. It features LG’s smart webOS platform with built-in streaming apps and allows Ethernet, Wi-Fi and Bluetooth connectivity. The picture is Full HD for sharper detail. Audio is modest (2 speakers at 5W each, Dolby Audio). The TV offers 2 HDMI inputs and 1 USB port for external devices.

  • Key Features:
  • 32″ Full HD LED panel (1920×1080) for crisp images.
  • Smart webOS interface with Wi-Fi, Ethernet, and Bluetooth support.
  • 2 HDMI ports and 1 USB port for connectivity.

Pros

  • Excellent sharpness: true Full HD resolution.
  • Smart connectivity: Ethernet port plus Wi-Fi/Bluetooth for streaming and screen sharing.

Cons

  • Weak audio: only 10W total (2×5W) speakers.
  • No HDR support (only standard picture mode).
  • Limited color volume (no advanced color tech like PurColor).

3. Samsung UA32T4380AKXXL (32″, T4380 Series)

Buy on Amazon

The Samsung UA32T4380AKXXL is a 32-inch HD Ready Smart LED TV with Samsung’s 4 Series design. It supports HDR10 and includes Samsung’s picture enhancements (Hyper Real Engine, Mega Contrast, PurColor, and Ultra/Digital Clean View) for better dynamic range. It also uses the Tizen OS (with SmartThings integration) for apps and smart features. Audio is 20W (2×10W) with Dolby Digital Plus. Like the T4450, it has 2 HDMI ports and 1 USB port.

  • Key Features:
  • 32″ LED (HD Ready, 1366×768) with Samsung HDR+picture enhancements (PurColor, Mega Contrast).
  • Tizen OS smart TV with Wi-Fi, Bluetooth and mobile mirroring.
  • 20W Dolby Digital Plus audio (2CH speakers).

Pros

  • Improved image: supports HDR10 and color enhancement (PurColor) for vivid visuals.
  • Smart features: same Tizen platform with Alexa/Google (as T4450) and SmartThings.
  • Decent sound: Dolby Digital Plus at 20W.

Cons

  • Only HD Ready resolution (less sharp than Full HD or 4K).
  • Similar performance to the T4450 but without Full HD; basic 60Hz refresh.

4. LG 32LR605B6LC (32″, LR60 Series)

Buy on Amazon

The LG 32LR605B6LC (LR60 series) is a 32-inch HD Ready Smart TV launched in 2025. It features LG’s α5 Gen6 AI Processor, which upscales content for clearer pictures and supports HDR10 and HLG formats. The TV runs webOS 23 with Wi-Fi 5 and Bluetooth 5.0 for streaming apps. Audio is 20W total (2×10W) with AI Sound virtual 5.1 upmix for surround effects. It has 2 HDMI ports (one with eARC) and 1 USB port.

  • Key Features:
  • 32″ LED (HD Ready) with HDR10/HLG and LG α5 Gen6 AI upscaling.
  • webOS 23 smart platform (Netflix, Prime, etc.) with Wi-Fi and Bluetooth.
  • 2×10W speakers with AI Sound (Virtual Surround) and eARC support.

Pros

  • Advanced processor: α5 Gen6 for better upscaling and detail.
  • Enhanced audio: virtual 5.1 surround and eARC connectivity.
  • Modern design: slim bezels and updated webOS 23 UI.

Cons

  • Only HD Ready (1366×768), so not as crisp as Full HD.
  • Lower contrast (no local dimming); performance similar to other budget 32″ TVs.

5. Samsung UA32T4360AKXXL (32″, T4360 Series)

Buy on Amazon

The Samsung UA32T4360AKXXL is a 32-inch entry-level HD Ready Smart TV. It offers basic smart TV functionality (Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, screen mirroring) and runs Samsung’s Tizen OS on a simpler panel. The display is 1366×768 (no HDR). Audio output is 20W (2×10W) from dual speakers. It includes 2 HDMI inputs and 1 USB port for connectivity.

  • Key Features:
  • 32″ LED (HD Ready, 1366×768) display.
  • Tizen smart TV with Wi-Fi, Bluetooth and screen mirroring.
  • 20W audio (2×10W) and standard Samsung smart UI.

Pros

  • Affordable smart TV: full web apps and Bluetooth TV audio.
  • Slim design: lightweight and easy to wall-mount.

Cons

  • No HDR support; basic picture quality only.
  • Only 10W per speaker (weaker audio).
  • No advanced processing or color enhancements (unlike higher models).

Conclusion

Choosing the best 32-inch smart TV in 2025 ultimately depends on your viewing habits, preferred features, and budget. From cutting-edge AI upscaling to intuitive operating systems and powerful built-in speakers, the models we’ve highlighted each bring something unique to the table. Whether you prioritize a snappy interface, smart home integration, or just exceptional picture quality in a compact form, there’s a perfect fit for you on this list.We hope this roundup has helped narrow down your options and made your buying decision a little easier.
